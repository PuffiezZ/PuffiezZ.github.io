Organic Runner is a fast-paced, single-player third-person shooter set in a cozy countryside village. The game features cute chibi-style art, energetic combat, and quick decision-making as players run, dodge, and fight their way through waves of enemies.
About the game
Organic Runner follows a young girl running a mini farm in a peaceful countryside village. Her crops grow at incredible speed, attracting many customers and bringing her great success. But jealousy arises, and some people attempt to shut down her booming business—forcing her to defend what she built.
Delivery
Players have 5 minutes to deliver crops. Each order appears at a random delivery location. When a delivery is completed, the player earns coins. Reach the required amount of coins to win!
Mechanics
The girl fires pumpkins as her projectiles. She moves quickly and has a charming, aesthetic style. She can also grow crops by pressing G to trigger a minigame. Once the progress bar is filled, wait a moment and the plants will sprout!
The thing I've done
In this project, my main role was Programming. I handled most of the gameplay systems using Blueprints. All mechanics and UI interactions were implemented with Blueprint scripting.

The AI was built using Unreal Engine’s built-in Behavior Tree system, designed with a combination of Behavior Tree and FSM. Aside from that, I also worked on Game Design and took the role of Project Manager.
