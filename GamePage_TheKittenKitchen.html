Award
One of the top 20 selected games showcased at TGS x Gamescom Asia.
The Kitten Kitchen is a single-player platformer featuring fast-paced cooking gameplay. It was selected as one of the 20 showcased games at the Thailand Game Show 2025. We released a demo during the event, and it received a lot of positive feedback.
About the game
The Kitten Kitchen follows a cat who ends up opening a bizarre restaurant after hiring a cheap architect who promptly runs away with his money. When the cat finally arrives at the building, he discovers that the restaurant is scheduled to open that very day, leaving him no choice but to start running it immediately.
the game include:
  • 3 major maps
  • 20 levels
  • 20+ recipe menu
  • Various of animal customers
  • Massive cute assets
Explore
Each level features its own unique design. The colors and textures change depending on the zone. When you complete a level, you earn stars that can be used to unlock the next one.
Mechanics
The character has ability to double jumps. Put the ingredient in the put and wait for second for finish food! Each map has it own ingredient, player chooses recipe menu wisely!
Serving
Customers will visit the restaurant, and the player must assign them a table. Once seated, customers will order from the menu. Your job is to take their orders and cook the correct dishes!
